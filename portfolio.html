During the talk on the topic of ‘An unfair crisis’, speaker Abi Adams-Prassl stated that during 2019, employment levels were at record highs in the UK. I used an API from the ONS website to see whether this was true or not. This is the highest value, however I could only find data between the years of 1971-2020, so my conclusion is subject to limitations
